About Me


I'm Aleesha. I'm a Full Stack Developer, based in London. I am a multi-disciplined web developer, specialising in all of the key areas required from concept, to building, launching and ensuring the success of a website. I enjoy building relationships with clients and collaborators. In early 2020, I completed Phase 1 (6 weeks) of a UX/UI Design course, I realised that wanted to revisit Software Development instead. Since I started the course, I have read through online material, signed up to regular coding newsletters, and watched online tutorials. Developing has been an opportunity to continuously learn new ways, update my skills, by producing high-quality software. Please refer to my page for further information and my Projects Showcase.
